About Our Church

Family Church of Praise has been serving our community for over 30 years, providing a warm and welcoming environment for believers and seekers alike.

We are a Bible-believing, Christ-centered church dedicated to worshiping God, building authentic relationships, and making a positive impact in our community.

Our mission is to help people discover their purpose, develop their faith, and deploy their gifts to serve others and glorify God.
